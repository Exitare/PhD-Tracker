About This Project

Built by someone who's been through the academic grind.

This tool was created by a PhD student who experienced firsthand how overwhelming the academic journey can be. With endless deadlines, scattered resources, and no streamlined way to set or track milestones, staying organized was a constant struggle.

Tools like OneNote, Notes, and spreadsheets helped for a while—but they quickly became cluttered, disjointed, and difficult to manage across projects. There was no single space where academic progress could be visualized and aligned with long-term goals.

That's why this tracker was born. It's a purpose-built solution designed to bring structure to the chaos, helping researchers focus less on managing the mess and more on making meaningful progress.

— From one academic to another
